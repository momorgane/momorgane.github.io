PM x Design x Dev: a trio in transition

The classic PM x Designer x Developer trio is supposed to work like a well-oiled machine… in reality, it’s often three people arguing over who broke the designs, who forgot the edge cases, and who keeps adding “just one more feature” to the MVP.

This talk cuts through the “AI builder” buzzword to look at what works in established organisations.

Leading design in challenging times

With budgets shrinking and the role of design under scrutiny, how do we stay impactful? Is the UX hype over? Doing more with less isn’t just a design problem.

This talk shares strategies from the business world to create impact with limited resources, protect our creative spaces and articulate value at scale.

We're not doing Design Systems properly and that’s okay

How do you ‘do’ Design Systems? Expectations are so high nowadays. Implement Design Tokens. Have a dedicated Design System team. Get a Design System manager.

There isn’t one way to do Design Systems. This talk shares some lessons on prioritising impacts over grip.
